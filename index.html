How the TTT Program Works

Who Will Train You?

At PGI, we believe that great leaders create more leaders. That’s why our Train the Trainer (TTT) program is conducted by our team of experienced master trainers. Their expertise ensures that every participant gains deep, impactful learning.

We’ve also shared reference links for further learning during the course.

When Is the Program Conducted?

The TTT Program is held every month and includes a qualifier test to maintain quality. Each batch includes only 20 trainees, ensuring personalized attention and a high-quality experience.

Where Is It Conducted?

We offer weekend sessions (Saturday & Sunday) for working professionals. Programs are available both offline and online, making them accessible across India and beyond.

How the Program Works?

Our experiential methodology includes games, storytelling, and activities.

First, participants go through a video series to build theoretical knowledge. Then, in live sessions, our master trainers share insights, answer doubts, and walk you through practical implementation using activity-based modules.
